About the company

Leading Manpower Export Company

Manpower Supply Agency International Limited is a leading manpower export company based in Bangladesh, dedicated to transforming the lives of skilled and semi-skilled laborers by providing them with international employment opportunities.

Our Goal

Our primary goal is to bridge the gap between the labor market in Bangladesh and the demand for skilled workers in various countries, thereby contributing to the economic growth of Bangladesh through substantial remittance earnings.

Our Mission

To empower individuals by providing them with the skills, training, and opportunities needed to secure meaningful employment overseas, thus improving their quality of life and supporting the development of their home country.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and reliable partner for global manpower solutions, recognized for our commitment to excellence, integrity, and the well-being of the workers we serve.
