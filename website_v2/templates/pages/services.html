Training Programs

We provide comprehensive training programs designed specifically for semi-skilled and unskilled workers. These programs aim to enhance their skills, making them more competitive in the global job market. Our training covers various trades and skills, ensuring workers are well-prepared for international employment opportunities.

Visa & Passport Processing

Our team handles all aspects of visa and passport processing, from initial applications to final approvals. We streamline the documentation process, ensuring compliance with all requirements and minimizing delays. Our goal is to provide a hassle-free experience for workers preparing to go abroad.

Pre-Departure Assistance

We offer extensive pre-departure assistance, including police clearance, medical certification, Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MoFA) approval, and fingerprint appointments. These services ensure that all necessary legal and health requirements are met, allowing workers to focus on their new opportunities abroad without concerns.

Flight Ticket Booking

We coordinate flight ticket bookings for our workers, arranging convenient travel plans to their overseas destinations. Our services ensure a smooth and stress-free journey, from the moment they leave home until they arrive at their new workplace, ready to start their job.

Job Placement

We partner with reputable international companies to place workers in various sectors, such as construction, electrical work, manufacturing, agriculture, domestic services, housekeeping, and nursing. Our job placement services match workers with suitable employers, ensuring a good fit and long-term employment success.
